# /source_material/control/01_non_goals.md

STATUS: CORE_CONTROL_DOC  
LOAD_PRIORITY: ALWAYS  
PURPOSE: Prevent scope drift and low-value implementation.

## HARD_NON_GOALS

[NG-001] Do not build a buzzword encyclopedia.

[NG-002] Do not build a passive course reader.

[NG-003] Do not build an LMS clone.

[NG-004] Do not build a quiz-first app.

[NG-005] Do not build a Multiple Choice app with decorative visuals.

[NG-006] Do not build a paper dump.

[NG-007] Do not build a prompt-engineering tips app.

[NG-008] Do not build generic AI news/trend content.

[NG-009] Do not build XP-driven gamification as the core product.

[NG-010] Do not let users randomly choose advanced topics before prerequisite models exist.

## CONTENT_NON_GOALS

[NG-020] Do not include a concept only because it is popular.

[NG-021] Do not explain frontier techniques without showing their system role.

[NG-022] Do not include research-paper details unless they support a practical architecture decision.

[NG-023] Do not teach implementation trivia before architecture purpose.

[NG-024] Do not overload lessons with long theory blocks.

[NG-025] Do not simplify so much that concepts become misleading.

## INTERACTION_NON_GOALS

[NG-030] Do not rely on exclusion-based questions.

[NG-031] Do not allow users to pass major tasks without understanding the core idea.

[NG-032] Do not use fake interactivity such as clicking “continue” after reading.

[NG-033] Do not make every task Multiple Choice.

[NG-034] Do not add drag-and-drop unless it improves learning and is technically stable.

[NG-035] Do not create interaction types that are used only once unless explicitly justified.

## VISUAL_NON_GOALS

[NG-040] Do not create one-off lesson-specific diagrams outside the visual system.

[NG-041] Do not use complex canvas graphics unless simpler SVG/HTML cannot work.

[NG-042] Do not prioritize visual impressiveness over readability.

[NG-043] Do not ship diagrams without mobile QA.

[NG-044] Do not allow overlapping labels, unreadable nodes, or hidden interaction targets.

## FEEDBACK_NON_GOALS

[NG-050] Do not psychologize user mistakes.

[NG-051] Do not say or imply “you made this mistake because you are a vibecoder.”

[NG-052] Do not use generic motivational feedback.

[NG-053] Do not say only “wrong” or “correct” for architecture tasks.

[NG-054] Do not critique the person. Analyze the system consequence of the decision.

## BUILD_NON_GOALS

[NG-060] Do not create monster components.

[NG-061] Do not hardcode lesson content inside UI components.

[NG-062] Do not duplicate source material across files.

[NG-063] Do not implement future phases before current phase gates pass.

[NG-064] Do not introduce Supabase before the persistence adapter is proven locally.
